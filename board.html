Our Board of Directors

Meet the dedicated volunteers leading our mission to connect rural children with specialized medical care

Our board members are passionate community leaders who donate their time and expertise to ensure every child in Sanpete County has access to the medical care they need. Together, they guide the Central Utah Tree Festival and oversee how funds are distributed to support families on their healthcare journeys.
