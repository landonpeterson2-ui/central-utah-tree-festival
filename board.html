Our Board of Directors

Meet the dedicated volunteers leading our mission to connect rural children with specialized medical care

Our board members are passionate community leaders who donate their time and expertise to ensure every child in Sanpete County has access to the medical care they need. Together, they guide the Central Utah Tree Festival and oversee how funds are distributed to support families on their healthcare journeys.

Photo

Viki Bailey

President

Viki Bailey is the President and Founder of the Central Utah Tree Festival, a nonprofit organization she established in 2024 to raise funds for children's medical needs while bringing the community together through a holiday tradition. As a nurse practitioner dedicated to pediatric care, Viki's professional experience caring for children and families inspired her to create this event, drawing on her love for the Primary Children's Festival of Trees and her passion for community service. She is a wife, mother of seven, and grandmother of five.

Markell Barclay

Vice President

Markell Barclay serves as Vice President of the organization and has spent the past few years supporting corporate fundraising, coordinating tree deliveries, and assisting with the legal and operational needs of the nonprofit. Having spent her entire life in Sanpete County—where her family has lived for generations—Markell has a deep connection to the community she serves. She believes in showing up, doing the work, and helping build something meaningful for the place she calls home.

Jill Carey

Online Presence and Social Media

Jill Carey lives in Ephraim and brings a wide range of life experience from having lived in many places around the world and throughout the United States. She loves staying active, especially when a workout is paired with good company and a lot of laughs. Jill enjoys spending time with her husband, family, and dogs, and she’s an avid reader who loves meaningful conversations about books.
