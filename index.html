Our Mission

When children in rural Sanpete County need specialized medical care, their families travel to Primary Children's Hospital in Salt Lake City‚Äîone of the nation's premier pediatric hospitals. These journeys add financial strain to already difficult times.

"The Central Utah Tree Festival helps cover travel costs for families making these critical trips."

Through our annual festival, we raise funds to ease the burden of these essential journeys, ensuring that no child is denied the premier care they need because of distance or financial hardship.
