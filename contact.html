Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for support?

Families in Sanpete County can apply for support through our online application form. We process applications quickly and work to provide assistance when it's needed most.

How can I volunteer?

We're always looking for volunteers to help with our annual festival, tree setup and delivery, event coordination, and more. Please email us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.

Can my business sponsor a tree?

Absolutely! Business sponsorships are a wonderful way to support our mission while gaining visibility in the community. Contact us to discuss sponsorship opportunities.

Where do donations go?

100% of donations go directly to supporting families. We provide assistance with gas, meals, parking, and lodging for families traveling to Primary Children's Hospital. We're an entirely volunteer-run organization with no paid staff.

When is the next festival?

Our festival typically takes place during the holiday season. Follow us on social media for updates on this year's event dates and details.
