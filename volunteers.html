Join Our Volunteer Team

Our festival runs 100% on volunteer power. Be part of something meaningful.

Why Volunteer?

When you volunteer with Central Utah Tree Festival, you're directly helping families in Sanpete County access critical medical care for their children. Every hour you give makes a difference.

Volunteer Roles

We have a variety of roles to match your interests and availability. No experience necessary!

Greeter / Welcome Desk

Be the friendly face that welcomes guests, answers questions, and directs visitors to key areas.

Auction Support

Help monitor silent auction items, assist bidders with questions, and support the checkout process.

Tree Guide

Monitor the decorated tree displays, guide guest flow, and help protect the beautiful creations.

Ticket Sales / Raffle

Sell admission and raffle tickets, handle payments, and share excitement about our prizes.

Setup Crew

Help arrange trees, set up tables and decorations, and prepare the venue before the festival opens.

Breakdown Crew

Pack up after the event, help auction winners claim their items, and restore the venue.

Volunteer FAQ

What's the minimum age to volunteer?

Volunteers must be at least 16 years old. Those under 18 must volunteer alongside a parent or guardian.

How long are volunteer shifts?

Most shifts are approximately 4 hours. We try to be flexible with scheduling to accommodate your availability.

Do I need any special training?

No prior experience is necessary! We'll provide all the training you need before your shift begins.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes. You'll receive a volunteer t-shirt to wear during your shift.

Can I volunteer with a group?

Absolutely! We welcome families, church groups, scout troops, and other organizations. Contact us to arrange group volunteering.

Will I receive community service hours?

Yes! We provide a community service hours letter upon request after you complete your volunteer shift.

Questions About Volunteering?

We're happy to answer any questions you might have about volunteer opportunities.
